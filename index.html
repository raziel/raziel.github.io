Google On-device speech recognition

I worked in the Google speech team to bring speech and related technologies to work entirely on-device. I was part of the team that developed the very-low-power "hey Google" capabilities across devices, and developed the first end-to-end system (and latest iteration of the ML model). I also developed the pre-TensorFlow ML inference engine that brought a new generation of speech recognizers, text-to-speech generators, and keyboard technology on-device.

This infrastructure, along with many techniques I implemented, promoted and refined --such as quantization, sparsity, and the replacement of vanilla LSTMs for Coupled Input-Forget Gate (CIFG) variant (introduced here)-- became the foudation of newer infrastructure and production models.
